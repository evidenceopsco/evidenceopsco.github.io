How it works

A repeatable evidence pipeline with clear gates and traceability — designed for fast-moving SaaS teams.

1) Collect

Exports, links, and screenshots with timestamps — gathered from the systems you already use.

2) Map & validate

Mapped to SOC 2 criteria (CC-series). We highlight missing artifacts and inconsistencies.

3) Package for auditors

Policy → procedure → evidence narratives, plus change diffs so each audit cycle is faster than the last.

Deliverables

Evidence pack (by control)

  • Evidence list (links/exports/screenshots)
  • Source system + timestamp
  • Evidence chain narrative (auditor-readable)
  • Notes that explain what each artifact proves

Gap backlog (actionable)

  • Exactly what’s missing (and why it matters)
  • Suggested owner (Security / IT / Eng / People Ops)
  • Priority + next step
  • Acceptance criteria for “done”
