How it works

A repeatable evidence pipeline with clear gates and traceability — designed for fast-moving SaaS teams.

1) Collect

We collect evidence from Google Admin, GitHub org settings, and Jira workflows — exports, links, and screenshots with timestamps.

2) Map & validate

Evidence is mapped to SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria (CC‑series). We highlight coverage, inconsistencies, and missing artifacts.

3) Package for auditors

We generate policy → procedure → evidence narratives, plus change diffs so each audit cycle is faster than the last.
