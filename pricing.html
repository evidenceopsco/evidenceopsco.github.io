Pricing (transparent structure)

We start with a fixed-scope Proof of Value. Then move to a monthly cadence if it works.

14‑day PoV

A fixed-scope engagement to produce a usable evidence pack sample.

  • 10 controls (SOC 2 CC‑series focus)
  • 3 systems: Google Workspace + GitHub + Jira
  • Deliverables: evidence pack sample + gap backlog + red-team notes
  • Response: within 1 business day after intake
Request PoV Download one‑pager Download deck

Monthly evidence ops (after PoV)

For teams that want evidence to stay fresh, consistent, and auditable.

  • Evidence refresh cadence (weekly / monthly)
  • Coverage & gaps tracked over time
  • Approval gates + audit trail maintained
  • Audit-season “accelerator” add‑on available

Pricing depends on number of systems, controls, and refresh frequency. We’ll propose a tier after PoV.

What we don’t do

To keep risk low and scope clear:

  • No production changes without explicit approval
  • No “we guarantee 0 findings” promises (we reduce surprises with evidence quality + red-team notes)
  • No need to paste secrets into documents
